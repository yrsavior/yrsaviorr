Hip-Hop Style Collection

Hair Style & Accessory

🔥 Hair Style Trends

Braids

Iconic hairstyle of hip-hop culture. Express your individuality with various styles like cornrows, box braids, and fishtails. Long-lasting and excellent for accessory coordination.

Dreadlocks

Originating from Jamaican Rastafarian culture and spreading to hip-hop. Creates a natural and rebellious image, with various changes possible depending on length and thickness.

Fade Cut

Style with gradually shorter sides and back. Divided into high fade, mid fade, and low fade, completing a clean yet trendy look.

Afro

Classic style with natural curly hair styled with volume. A symbol of traditional Black culture since the 70s, still consistently loved in modern times.

Cornrows

Braided style tightly woven close to the scalp. Creative expression possible with geometric patterns, practical style favored by athletes.

Twist

Style created by twisting two strands of hair together. Includes flat twist, two-strand twist, etc., creating a natural yet sophisticated feel.

💎 Accessory Trends

Chains

Essential item for hip-hop style. Various styling possible depending on thickness, length, and pendant. Express luxury with materials like gold, silver, and platinum.

Grillz

Decorative accessories worn on teeth. Made with gold, diamonds, etc., iconic item of hip-hop artists symbolizing wealth and success.

Bandana

Multi-purpose accessory worn on head, neck, arms, etc. Individual expression possible with various patterns and colors, enhancing the completeness of casual street looks.

Caps & Beanies

Various hats including snapbacks, fitted caps, beanies. Express belonging with brand logos or team marks, or create minimal looks with simple designs.

Sunglasses

Key item for creating a cool image. Various styles like oversized, aviator, wayfarer emphasize the artist's individuality and charisma.

Earrings & Piercings

Earrings and piercings in various designs like studs, hoops, drops. Express luxury and individuality simultaneously with diamonds or unique designs.
